## **Shop System**

This contains info related to the shop system.

### _Weapon Shop_

- Sells Weapons
- List of Weapons includes
  - Swords
  - Axe
  - Rod
  - Spear
  - Knife
  - Shield

### _Armor Shop_

- Sells Armor
- List of Armor includes
  - Helmet
  - ChestPiece
  - Boots
  - Gauntlet

### _Item Shop_

- Sells Items
- List if Items includes
  - Potions
  - Ethers
  - Tent
  - Paralyze Heal
  - Awakening
  - Revive

### _Motel_

- Motel for healing characters
- Also heals fainted characters
